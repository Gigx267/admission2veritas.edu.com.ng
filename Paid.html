Student - Payment History Help Tips
  1. Making a New Payment:
    1. Click on the "Make Payment" button.
    2. You will be redirected to the payment category page where you can select your desired payment plan.
  2. Viewing Previous Payment History:
    1. A list of all your previous payments will be displayed on the payment history page.
    2. You can click on any entry to view detailed information and print a receipt if needed.
